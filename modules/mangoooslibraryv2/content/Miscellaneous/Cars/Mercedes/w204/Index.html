W204 C63 (Pre-Facelift)

The Mercedes-Benz W204 C63 AMG is the high-performance variant of the W204 C-Class produced from 2008 to 2011 before the facelift update. Powered by the naturally aspirated 6.2L M156 V8 engine, it became known for its raw driving characteristics, aggressive exhaust note, and relatively simple AMG drivetrain compared to later turbocharged models.

The W204 C63 has the best sounding V8 in the world. Especially with the long tube headers modification. Not only does the car look amazing, the sound is just the cherry on top. By far my favorite car when it comes to sound.

Modifications:

- Long tube headers
- ECU tune
- Secondary cat delete

Additional modifications:

- AMG Performance Exhaust

Known issues:

- Engine (M156 6.2L V8)
- Head bolt failures (early production models)
- Camshaft and lifter wear
- Intake manifold runner flap failure

- Transmission (7G-Tronic AMG Speedshift)
- Conductor plate failures
- Harsh shifting under load
- Transmission fluid connector leaks

- Suspension and Steering
- Front control arm bushing wear
- Rear subframe bushing deterioration
- Adaptive suspension component wear (if equipped)

- Electrical / Electronics
- Steering lock (ESL) failure
- COMAND system freezing
- Parking sensor malfunctions

- Brakes / Tires
- Rapid rear tire wear
- Expensive AMG brake replacement
- Brake disc cracking under heavy use
