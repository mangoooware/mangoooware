Known issues:

- (2003 to 2006) Model
- Supercharger failures / oil leaks
- Balance shaft issues
- Camshaft position sensor failures

- Transmission (5G-Tronic 5-speed)
- Valve body wear / rough shifting
- Torque converter shudder

- Suspension and Steering
- Front lower control arm bushings
- Ball joints and sway bar links
- Air suspension failures (if equipped)

- Electrical / Electronics
- COMAND system glitches
- Instrument cluster pixel issues
- Sunroof and window regulator failures

- Brakes / Tires
- High maintenance cost for AMG brakes
- Fast tire wear due to power and camber
